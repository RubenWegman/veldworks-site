Contact

Work is selective.
Inquiry is open.

VeldWorks works with a small number of organisations at any given time. Before reaching out, the questions below are worth sitting with.

Are you ready?

Not a checklist in the conventional sense. These are the signals that indicate an organisation is positioned for genuine engagement — not reassurance, not a framework, not an AI strategy deck.

Leadership is asking structural questions, not performance questions
Decision cycles feel like a liability rather than a safeguard
The organisation suspects its scale is producing drag more than advantage
There is willingness to stop doing work that only exists to coordinate other work
A small, decisive engagement is acceptable — broad participation is not required

Not the right fit if:

You need an AI strategy deck before naming a business constraint
Consensus and alignment are the required outputs
The work needs to be politically safe inside the institution
Governance is treated as proof of seriousness

Get in touch

reinout@veldworks.nl Send a message

There is no form and no intake call to schedule. Send a message describing the constraint — not the solution you have in mind. The clearer the constraint, the faster the response.

VeldWorks works with organisations across Know, Guide, and Build. If you are unsure which mode is relevant, that is a reasonable place to start the conversation.

Response time: typically within two working days.
